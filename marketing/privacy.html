01 —

Who We Are

UpBeat is a prototype for a music requesting platform for bars and venues across the UK. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website or waitlist form.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, UpBeat is the data controller responsible for your personal information.

Please note that UpBeat is an early-stage project which is being developed as part of a third year module at Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen, Scotland. This website is only used to understand interest in the product and to contact people who would like updates about its development.

If you have any questions about this notice or how we handle your data, please contact us at o.meade@rgu.ac.uk.

02 —

What Data We Collect

When you sign up to our waitlist, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your organisation name
  • Your role (e.g. bartender, venue owner, DJ), if provided

We do not collect any sensitive personal data, payment information, or data from minors. We do not use cookies beyond what is strictly necessary to operate this website.

03 —

How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used solely to:

  • Add you to our early access waitlist
  • Send you updates about UpBeat's launch and availability
  • Occasionally share relevant product news or announcements

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We will never use your data for anything you wouldn't reasonably expect.

04 —

How Long We Keep Your Data

We will retain your waitlist information for as long as UpBeat is in development and for a reasonable period after launch. If you ask us to remove your data at any time, we will do so promptly - usually within 30 days.

05 —

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access - you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification - you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - you can ask us to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing - you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object - you can object to us processing your data
  • Right to withdraw consent - at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at o.meade@rgu.ac.uk.

06 —

Data Security

Form submissions are stored securely using Google Forms and Google Sheets. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to those who need it to operate the service.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08 —

Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time as UpBeat grows. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. If any changes are significant, we'll let you know by email where possible.
