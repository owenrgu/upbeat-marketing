What is UpBeat?

The crowd
runs the
dancefloor.

UpBeat puts music control in the hands of your customers. No more shouting over the bar - they browse, request, and vote from their phones. You set the rules, they set the vibe.

Features

Built for the vibes.

🎵

Live Song Requests

Customers search and request any song in seconds, adding to the playlist in real-time.

⚔️

Vote Battles

Vote for your favourite song to influence the playlist. Songs with more votes will be played sooner!

📊

Live Queue

For full transparency: everyone sees what's up next and how many votes each track has.

🎛️

Bar Controls

Owners keep full control - block songs, set request limits, and override the queue whenever needed. Customers pick from pre-approved songs, so the vibe stays exactly how you like it.

⚡

Instant Setup

Register your venue in minutes. Guests connect via QR code or directly through the mobile app - zero friction, maximum engagement.

📈

Venue Analytics

See what your crowd loves. Track top requested songs, busiest hours, and crowd engagement trends.

How It Works

Three steps.
Infinite vibes.

01
Bar scans in with a QR code

Register your venue, set your preferences, and display the QR code on your tables or bar top. You control everything from the songs you want to play to the user request limit.

02
Guests request & vote instantly

Customers scan, search for a song, submit a request, or jump into a live vote battle - right from their phone.

03
The highest votes play next

UpBeat surfaces the most popular songs to the top of the playlist. The crowd decides, the energy speaks for itself.
